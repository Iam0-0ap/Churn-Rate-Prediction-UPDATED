About Us

We are CoderNP. Just a short time ago, we were fascinated by the idea of predicting the customer churn. We decided to build a fully functional Churn Prediction System using Machine Learning. Since the cost for the company which results to adding a new customer is far more than the cost of retaining the customer within the network, customer churn rate predictor is a must have tool for such subscription based bussiness models.
Our mission is to help the telecom industry reduce the customer attrition/churn.
